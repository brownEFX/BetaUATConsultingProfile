Our Objectives

  1. Why ​do I want to learn this skill?

    2. What are your motivations?
    3. Will you prove something to yourself or people around you?
    4. Will you get a raise because you’re better at your job or will you get a better job?

  2. What will I​ achieve ​if I learn this skill?
    1. Which doors will this skill open for you?
    2. Will you be able to earn more?
    3. Will you be able to have more flexibility in your work hours?
    4. Will you be able to work remotely and travel more?

  3. How will this skill ​change mylife ​and my career?
    1. Would you get a raise at your current job?
    2. How would your life change?

  4. How will I ​feel ​if I never accomplish this?
    1. Would you feel disappointed?
    2. Would you feel like you’ve messed up and missed out?

  5. What would ​my life ​look like if I manage to accomplish this?
    1. Visualise it.
    2. What would life look like from the moment you wake up to the time you go to sleep?
